The part everyone misses

For five years, Law 151 was a law on paper that no one had to obey. In 2025, that changed.

In plain wordsA law usually cannot be enforced until the government issues its “executive regulations”, the detailed rulebook. Egypt passed Law 151 in 2020, but the rulebook only arrived in November 2025. So the real obligations, licences, and fines start to bite now, in 2026.

Data leaving Egypt

Sending data abroad? Three gates.

In plain wordsYou cannot freely move Egyptians' data abroad. The destination must protect data at least as well as Egypt, you need a PDPC licence first, and the PDPC has 90 days to decide, silence counts as NO.
