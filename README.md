# Egypt Personal Data Protection Law (151/2020), Interactive Presentation

A bilingual (English / Arabic), interactive slide presentation on **Egypt's Personal Data Protection Law No. 151 of 2020** and its **2025 Executive Regulations**, built for an EMBA "Information Technology Strategy" assignment.

**Live presentation:** https://amsamms.github.io/egypt-pdp-law-151/

## What is inside

- `index.html`, a self-contained 19-slide interactive deck you navigate left / right (arrow keys, swipe, on-screen buttons, or dots). It includes:
  - a live countdown to the ~November 2026 enforcement deadline,
  - an interactive timeline (2020 to 2026),
  - flip-card key definitions,
  - a "which lawful basis applies?" picker (Article 6),
  - a penalty explorer with a repeat-offence toggle,
  - a "simulate a breach" 72-hour timer,
  - an Egypt-vs-GDPR comparison with a highlight-differences toggle,
  - an animated PDPC role wheel, sector tabs, a 5-question quiz, and a live compliance-readiness ring.
  - a full **English / Arabic** language toggle with right-to-left layout.
- `downloads/Egypt_PDPL_151-2020_EN.pptx`, the matching English PowerPoint deck.
- `downloads/Egypt_PDPL_151-2020_AR.pptx`, the matching Arabic PowerPoint deck.

## The headline

Law 151 was passed in 2020 but sat largely dormant for five years because it had no executive regulations. Those regulations were issued by **Prime Ministerial Decree No. 816 on 1 November 2025** (in force 2 November 2025), starting a one-year compliance clock. Enforcement begins around **November 2026**, which is why the law matters now.
